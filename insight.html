Weather Dashboard

The Weather Dashboard offers real-time weather updates through an interactive map, enabling users to easily access current conditions and make informed decisions for their daily activities or travel plans.

Terms used:

  • Clouds: Shows the cloud coverage and the types of clouds present in the sky.
  • Humidity: Represents the amount of moisture in the air, expressed as a percentage (%), indicating how muggy or dry it feels.
  • Precipitation: Measures the amount of rainfall, snow, or other forms of moisture in the atmosphere.
  • Pressure: Shows atmospheric pressure, which helps predict weather changes.
  • Sunrise: The time at which the sun appears above the horizon in the morning, marking the beginning of the day.
  • Sunset: The time when the sun disappears below the horizon in the evening, signaling the end of the day.
  • Temperature: Displays the current temperature and forecasts for upcoming days.
  • Visibility: The distance one can clearly see, measured in kilometers (km), which can be affected by weather conditions like fog or rain.
  • Wind: Indicates wind speed and direction, important for weather forecasting.
  • Wind Speed: Measures how fast the air is moving, typically in kilometers per hour (km/h), indicating how breezy or windy it is.

Understanding Traffic Conditions

Our traffic page provides real-time updates on vehicle speed, free-flow speed for optimal conditions, and a confidence percentage to ensure data reliability. The traffic condition field offers an overview, helping users make informed travel decisions and navigate smoothly.
