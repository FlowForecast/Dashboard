About Our Company

Our Mission

At Flow & Forecast, Our mission is to empower individuals to make smarter, more informed decisions by providing real-time, accurate, and comprehensive weather, event, and traffic updates. By delivering personalized insights on local conditions and happenings, we aim to enhance everyday life, reduce travel stress, and create seamless experiences for users navigating their cities. Whether it's planning your commute, attending an event, or staying prepared for changing weather, we are committed to helping our users stay ahead and enjoy their day with confidence.

API Usage

We use Openweathermap API for our Weather Dashboard. Openweathermap Website
We use TomTom Traffic API for our Traffic Dashboard. TomTom Traffic Website
We use PredictHq API for our Local Events Dashboard. PredictHq Website
We use IPgeolocation API for the Geolocation. IPgeolocation Website
